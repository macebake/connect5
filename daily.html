How to Play Connect 5 Daily

Connect all 5 starting tiles using words within 6 turns.

  • Click an empty cell to start typing
  • Use arrow keys to move your typing position
  • Click the same cell again to change direction
  • Words must connect to existing tiles
  • Earn Scrabble letter points + 10 per unused turn
  • Starting tiles are black, committed tiles are gray
  • Words validated against American English dictionary

Daily Challenge Rules

  • Same puzzle for everyone: All players get identical starting tiles
  • Play without signing in: Anyone can play today's puzzle
  • Sign in to compete: Save your score and appear on the leaderboard
  • One attempt per account: Signed-in users cannot restart
  • New puzzle at midnight GMT
