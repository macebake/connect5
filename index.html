How to Play Connect 5

Connect all 5 starting tiles using words within 6 turns.

  • Click an empty cell to start typing.
  • Use arrow keys to move your starting point before you type.
  • Click the same cell again to switch between horizontal and vertical.
  • Every word must connect to the existing letters on the board.
  • Score is based on letter values plus a bonus for unused turns.
  • The daily puzzle is the same for everyone on that date.
