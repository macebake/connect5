How to Play Connect 5 Daily

Connect all 5 starting tiles using words within 6 turns.

  • Click an empty cell to start typing.
  • Use arrow keys to move your starting point before you type.
  • Click the same cell again to switch direction.
  • On mobile, tapping a cell opens the keyboard and hides some chrome while you type.
  • Words must connect to existing letters.
  • Your turns are only dictionary-checked when all five starting tiles are connected.
  • Reset restarts the current puzzle from the beginning.
  • Earn letter points plus 10 bonus points per unused turn.
  • Starting tiles are black and committed tiles are gray.
  • The daily puzzle is the same for everyone each day and resets at 00:00 UTC.
