How Ranking Works

Daily Leaderboard

  • Top 10 scores from today's puzzle
  • Ranked by score (highest first)
  • Tiebreaker: earliest completion time
  • Updates live throughout the day
  • Frozen at midnight GMT for historical record

All-Time Champions

  • Total leaderboard appearances
  • Only counts if you finish in daily top 10
  • Ranked by total appearances
  • Shows true consistency over time

Scoring System

Letter Points: Standard Scrabble values (A=1, Q=10, etc.)
Bonus Points: +10 for each unused turn (if you win)
Multiple Words: All intersecting words count toward your score
